#  Customer Behavior Analysis & Power BI Dashboard

## Problem Statement

A leading retail company wants to better understand its customers’ shopping behavior to improve **sales performance, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty**.

The management team has observed changes in purchasing patterns across:
- Demographics (age group, gender)
- Product categories
- Subscription behavior
- Payment methods
- Shipping preferences
- Seasonal trends
- Discounts and promotions

They are particularly interested in understanding **which factors influence customer decisions and repeat purchases**, such as discounts, reviews, seasons, and payment preferences.

###  Business Question

> **How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?**

##  Dataset Overview

The dataset contains customer-level transaction and behavior data, including:

- Customer ID
- Age & Age Group
- Gender & Location
- Item Purchased & Product Category
- Purchase Amount
- Purchase Frequency
- Previous Purchases
- Review Rating
- Subscription Status
- Discount Applied
- Payment Method
- Shipping Type
- Season

##  Methodology

### 1️ Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Standardized column names
- Handled missing values
- Created derived columns (Age Group, Purchase Frequency Days)
- Exported clean dataset to CSV for visualization

### 2️ Data Analysis
- Revenue and sales analysis by demographics
- Customer segmentation based on purchase behavior
- Subscription vs non-subscription comparison
- Category-wise and age-group-wise analysis

### 3️ Dashboard Development (Power BI)
- Created DAX measures for KPIs
- Built interactive visuals
- Added slicers for dynamic filtering
- Designed a business-friendly dashboard layout

##  Key Insights

- Subscription customers show stronger engagement
- Clothing and Accessories generate the highest revenue
- Middle-aged and Young Adult customers are the most valuable segments
- Higher review ratings correlate with repeat purchases
- Shipping and discount strategies influence purchase frequency

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing campaigns on high-value age groups
- Strengthen subscription-based offers to improve loyalty
- Use customer reviews as a conversion driver
- Optimize category-level promotions based on performance
- Personalize shipping and discount strategies
